CAFfeZeit

CAFfe-Zeit is a project that has emerged from the many trainings, hackathons and workshops around the Microsoft Cloud Adoption Framework CAF in the last twelve months. The CAF is such an extensive work and there are so many aspects around the CAF. Many participants from my trainings supported the idea of a “CAF round”. That’s why I just started the “CAFfe-Zeit” (I know, it works better in German).A community of CAF interested people who want to exchange ideas regularly, have discussions or simply consume a few news. For this I have set up this small website to provide the results of this CAFfe time.

I have already done a small survey among the participants of my trainings and built up a first backlog of topics for the CAFfe time. “Stay tuned” first content will come soon…

I am happy to include further ideas, topics or questions in the backlog! Go to the public CAFfeZeit Repo Discussion on Github and participate in the discussion. We will also be happy to invite active members of the community to write posts, record videos or simply be mentioned as a source for an idea.

I look forward to your active participation!

Have fun
